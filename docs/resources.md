We have a wide variety of samples to choose from:

* "Vendor applications" and "Standalone IFC Samples" provide some
examples that show how to implement some popular software packages such as Revit, AecoSim, Sketchup, and other tools. These guides
try to serve as canonical walkthroughs that help you use familiar packages with IFC.

* "Multi Application Samples" are generally of a more contrived and instructional nature, highlighting key concepts that
you'll encounter when developing with IFC, and teaching some principles of building IFC applications.

## Recently updated samples

The IFC team is highlighting these samples as being the most up-to-date. Active upkeep is being done or has recently been done
on these samples to ensure they are high quality and work on IFC {{page.version}}.
