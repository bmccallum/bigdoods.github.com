IFC classes

The "classes of objects" allow to describe and qualify all the elements of the building (walls, slabs, partitions, doors, windows, spaces, equipments ...) and their relations (between a wall and a window, Equipment and premises).

BuildingSmart mark of approval

IFC Features

Automatic Feature

Automatic constraints, while not sacrificing availability. Mix critical and best-effort workloads in order to drive up utilization and save even more resources.

Semantic object linking

Connects classes to ensure accurate mapping between objects using advanced Express features.

AP classes

IFC progressively makes use of the AP classes developed by the international standards organisation. Take advantage of a growing ecosystem of interoperability solutions.
